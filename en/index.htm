FAQ

How do I participate in the retreat?

Pay in advance to participate and come 🙂

What are the living arrangements?

We ask you to take care of your own accommodation during the retreat.
Please write to us and we will recommend hotels/hostels near the retreat site.

What about food?

During the retreat there will be a cafe where you can buy tea, coffee, water and light snacks. There is a pizzeria, McDonald’s, and grocery stores not far from the retreat.

Can I take my child to the retreat?

Yes, but be sure to register your child. During the preaching sessions at the retreat, there will be a children’s program for children from 4 years old to 12 years old. Care for children under 4 years old must be arranged by parents.

Why pre-register?

People with registrations go through a quick check-in on the spot, without a general queue.
And if the number of participants exceeds the allowed maximum, the registration will be closed.

Where will the retreat take place?

April 18-20.
Warsaw, 20 Wyborna str.
